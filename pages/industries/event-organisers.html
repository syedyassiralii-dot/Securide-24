EXPLORE FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT SECURITY SUPPORT DO EVENT ORGANISERS TYPICALLY NEED?

Event organisers need threat assessment, venue and access controls, movement planning, live monitoring, and escalation-ready incident response to protect attendees and operations.

CAN SECURIDE 24 SUPPORT DIFFERENT EVENT TYPES?

Yes. Support is tailored for conferences, summits, corporate events, public gatherings, and high-profile programmes with varying audience size and risk profile.

HOW IS LIVE EVENT RISK MONITORED?

We maintain situational awareness through continuous monitoring, on-ground coordination, and escalation protocols that allow rapid response to emerging issues.

HOW ARE VIP AND SPEAKER MOVEMENTS MANAGED?

Through dedicated movement planning, route control, timed access, and coordinated liaison to protect principals while preserving event flow and protocol.

WHAT HAPPENS IF AN INCIDENT OCCURS DURING AN EVENT?

We activate pre-planned response protocols, coordinate stakeholders, and support rapid stabilisation actions to protect people, continuity, and brand reputation.

CAN SUPPORT START DURING LATE-STAGE EVENT PREPARATION?

Yes. We can engage at late planning stages and prioritise critical controls, live monitoring, and contingency readiness for upcoming events.
